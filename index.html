    Mobile work works: The ROI of mobility

    It’s no accident that organizations are increasingly enabling their people to connect from outside the office. Employers derive at least as much value from remote work as employees do.

    Organizations adopting mobile workstyles cite happier, more productive employees and lower costs.

    Each remote worker has been estimated to produce 240 extra hours of work per year.
